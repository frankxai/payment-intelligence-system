## What

H402 is an independent HTTP 402 machine-to-machine payment protocol developed by bit-gpt. It implements the `402 Payment Required` semantics for APIs, compute, and data services without affiliation to the x402 Foundation or Lightning Labs. H402 supports configurable payment backends.

## Who

Developer: **bit-gpt** (GitHub organization). Independent of x402 Foundation and Lightning Labs.

## Mechanism

H402 follows the general HTTP 402 pattern:
1. Client requests resource
2. Server responds 402 with payment instructions and supported backend types
3. Client selects backend (PSP, crypto, etc.), makes payment
4. Client presents payment proof to server
5. Server verifies and grants access

The key difference from x402: H402 does not presuppose a specific facilitator or blockchain. It is backend-agnostic by design, allowing operators to plug in any payment processor.

## When to choose

- You want HTTP 402 semantics without dependency on x402 Foundation infrastructure
- Your use case requires a specific payment backend not supported by x402 Foundation facilitators
- You need M2M payment gating with maximum PSP flexibility

**Not for:** When you specifically need x402 Foundation network effects, Lightning liquidity, or card-network consumer protection.
